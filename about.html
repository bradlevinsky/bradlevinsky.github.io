About FixIt Forward

FixIt Forward is a grassroots initiative focused on connecting homeowners in need with compassionate mentors from the skilled trades. Whether you're facing financial challenges, struggling with repairs, or simply need guidance, our platform brings neighbors together to support one another.

Who We Help

  • Single parents juggling costs and repairs
  • Retired individuals living on fixed incomes
  • People with disabilities or health limitations
  • Anyone who wants to learn or get help without judgment

How It Works

  1. Homeowners register and list what kind of help they need
  2. Mentors list their trades and availability
  3. Users are matched based on skills, location, and need
  4. Both parties benefit through support, learning, and community

Why We Exist

For many people, keeping their homes safe and functional feels out of reach. FixIt Forward was built to empower people, not only by providing help but by offering mentorship that builds lasting knowledge and connection. We believe housing should be stable, dignified, and supported by community.

Our Founder

Brad Levinsky created FixIt Forward after working in both the HVAC and renovation industries. He witnessed firsthand how difficult it can be for homeowners to get affordable, trustworthy help, and how many skilled retirees still want to give back. Brad launched this project to connect those two groups in a way that’s practical, empowering, and filled with heart.
